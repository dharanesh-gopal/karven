## ğŸ¨ Design Tips

### Text Readability:
- **Font Size:** Minimum 40px for titles, 24px for subtitles
- **Font Weight:** Bold or semi-bold
- **Contrast:** Dark text on light background (or vice versa)
- **Shadow:** Add drop shadow to text over images

### Colors:
- Use your brand colors
- Background: Single color or subtle gradient
- Avoid: Too many colors (stick to 2-3)

### Images:
- High quality (no pixelation)
- Relevant to page content
- Not too busy (keep it simple)
- Proper licensing (use free stock: unsplash.com, pexels.com)
