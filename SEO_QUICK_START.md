## ğŸ“– SEO Field Reference

### Meta Title (Required)
- **Length:** 50-60 characters
- **Example:** "KarVenSen | Drone Services & AI Development"
- **Tip:** Include company name + what you do

### Meta Description (Required)
- **Length:** 150-160 characters
- **Example:** "Transform your business with expert drone technology, AI development, and cloud solutions. Professional training available."
- **Tip:** Make people want to click

### Keywords (Recommended)
- **Count:** 5-10 keywords
- **Example:** drone services, AI development, cloud solutions
- **Tip:** Think "What would I Google to find this?"

### OG Image (Recommended)
- **Size:** 1200 x 630 pixels
- **When:** Creates nice previews when shared on social media
- **How:** Use Canva (see OG_IMAGE_CREATION_GUIDE.md)

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "I can't find SEO Settings in Sanity"
- Make sure you're editing the correct page (e.g., "Home Page Settings", not just "Home")
- Scroll down - SEO is usually at the bottom

### "My OG image isn't showing on Facebook"
- Use Facebook Debugger to refresh
- Make sure image is exactly 1200x630px
- Check file size is under 1MB

### "Google still shows old title/description"
- Google caches data. Can take 1-4 weeks to update
- Use Google Search Console to request re-indexing
